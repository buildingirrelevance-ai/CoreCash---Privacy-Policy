1. What CoreCash Does

CoreCash™ is a mobile utility app that helps auto mechanics and DIY car enthusiasts track core deposit return deadlines for auto-parts purchases. The App lets you:

  • Photograph parts-store receipts for AI-powered core charge extraction
  • Manually enter core deposit information
  • Set and receive deadline reminder notifications
  • Track which cores have been returned and calculate money recovered

CoreCash offers a free trial and several paid tiers (one-time purchases and monthly/annual subscriptions). Full pricing details are in Section 3.

Important: CoreCash™ is not responsible for missed core return deadlines. You are solely responsible for verifying and entering accurate return dates. CoreCash provides receipt scanning and reminder services only and makes no guarantee that information extracted from receipts is accurate. Always verify your return deadline directly with your receipt or by contacting the store.

3.2 How AI scans are counted

  • Free Trial: You may create up to 3 cores total, of which a maximum of 1 may use AI scanning. Both AI scans and manual entries count toward the 3-core limit.
  • Top-Up only (no Pro Unlock or Shop Plan): Each core you create — whether by AI scan or manual entry — deducts 1 from your scan balance. Buying any Top-Up removes the free-tier 3-core cap.
  • Pro Unlock: Manual entries are unlimited and do not deduct anything. AI scans deduct 1 from your bundled 200-scan balance. After the bundled 200 are used, AI scans deduct from any Top-Up balance you have purchased.
  • Shop Plans: Manual entries are unlimited and do not deduct anything. AI scans deduct 1 from your current cycle allowance (300 or 1,000 depending on tier). If you hit your monthly cap, additional AI scans deduct from any Top-Up balance you have purchased; manual entry remains unlimited.

3.3 Top-Up balance behavior

Top-Up purchases add a permanent balance to your account that survives:

  • Subscription cancellation
  • Subscription plan changes (upgrade or downgrade)
  • Subscription expiration or non-renewal
  • App reinstall on the same Google Play account

Top-Up balance is reduced only by AI scans (when you have a Pro Unlock or active Shop Plan) or by any core creation (when you have no other paid entitlement). Top-Up balance is never reduced by manual entry under Pro Unlock or Shop Plan, since manual entry is already unlimited under those plans. Top-Up balance does not expire.

4. AI Receipt Scanning

The receipt scanning feature transmits your receipt image from your device to our backend (hosted on Google Cloud), which forwards the image to Anthropic's Claude API for text extraction. By using this feature, you acknowledge that:

  • AI extraction is not guaranteed to be 100% accurate. You are responsible for verifying all extracted information before saving.
  • Receipt images are transmitted over encrypted HTTPS and are not permanently stored by us or by Anthropic.
  • You must not upload images that contain illegal content, depict minors, or violate the rights of others.
  • You must not use the scanning feature in any way that violates Anthropic's Usage Policies.
  • The scanning service depends on the availability of third-party APIs. We do not guarantee uninterrupted access.
