CoreCash™ by Building Irrelevance

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 13, 2026
The short version: CoreCash stores all your data locally on your device. We never sell your data. The only information that leaves your device is receipt photos sent to our AI provider for scanning — and those are not stored permanently. That's it.

1. Who We Are

CoreCash™ is a mobile app developed by Building Irrelevance, based in Dothan, Alabama. CoreCash helps auto mechanics and DIY car enthusiasts track core deposit return deadlines by scanning parts store receipts using AI.

Questions? Contact us: buildingirrelevance@gmail.com

2. What Information We Collect

CoreCash collects only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Receipt images — When you use the AI scanner, the photo you choose is sent to our AI provider (Anthropic) to extract core charge information. The image is processed and immediately discarded — it is not stored permanently by us or by Anthropic.
  • Core deposit data you enter — Part names, store names, dollar amounts, and return dates that you enter manually or that are extracted from your receipt. This data is stored locally on your device only.
  • App preferences — Your notification settings. Stored locally on your device only.

We do not collect: your name, email address, phone number, location, payment information, or any account credentials. CoreCash has no user accounts and no login.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Receipt scanning: Receipt images are sent to the Anthropic Claude API to extract core charge amounts, store names, part names, and return deadlines. Results are returned to your device and the image is discarded.
  • Deadline reminders: If you enable notifications, CoreCash uses the return dates you entered to schedule local push notifications on your device. These are generated entirely on your device — no data is sent to any server to trigger them.
  • App functionality: Your locally stored core deposit data is used solely to display information within the app.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. The only third party that receives any data is:

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — Receipt images are transmitted over encrypted HTTPS to Anthropic's API for processing. Anthropic does not permanently store these images. You can review Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy.

5. Data Storage and Retention

All core deposit records, notification preferences, and scan counts are stored locally on your device. We do not operate a server or database that holds your data.

Your data is retained on your device until you delete individual records within the app or uninstall the app. Uninstalling CoreCash removes all locally stored data.

6. How We Keep Your Information Safe

  • All data stays on your device — no external database exists
  • Receipt images are transmitted to Anthropic over encrypted HTTPS only
  • No user accounts or passwords to compromise
  • No server-side storage means no server-side breach risk

7. Device Permissions

  • Camera / Storage: Required to photograph or upload receipt images for AI scanning. You may revoke this at any time in your device settings — manual entry still works without it.
  • Notifications: Required to send deadline reminders. You may disable notifications at any time in your device settings or within the CoreCash Settings screen.

8. Children's Privacy

CoreCash is intended for users 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has used the app, contact us at buildingirrelevance@gmail.com.

9. Your Privacy Rights

Because all data is stored on your device, you have direct control at all times:

  • Delete any core record using the three-dot menu (⋮) on any card
  • Delete all data by uninstalling the app
  • Revoke camera access in your device settings
  • Disable notifications in your device settings or in the CoreCash Settings screen

Residents of California, Colorado, Connecticut, Texas, Virginia, and other states with enacted privacy laws may have additional rights including the right to know, access, correct, or delete personal data. Since we hold no server-side data about you, most of these rights are automatically satisfied by your local device control. For any requests, contact us at buildingirrelevance@gmail.com.

10. California "Shine the Light" Law

We do not share personal information with third parties for direct marketing purposes. California residents may contact us to confirm this at buildingirrelevance@gmail.com.

11. Do Not Track

CoreCash does not track your browsing activity, use advertising identifiers, or respond to Do-Not-Track signals. No behavioral or advertising tracking of any kind occurs within this app.

12. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when we add new features or as required by law. Updates will be noted by a revised date at the top of this page and communicated through app update release notes on Google Play.
