This Privacy Notice for Building Irrelevance ("we," "us," or "our") describes how and why we might access, collect, store, use, and/or share ("process") your personal information when you use our services ("Services"), including when you:

In Short: We process your information to provide, improve, and administer our Services and communicate with you.

We process your personal information for the following reasons:

  • To respond to user inquiries and offer support. We may process your information to respond to your questions or support requests.

3. WHEN AND WITH WHOM DO WE SHARE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?

In Short: We may share information in specific situations described in this section.

We may need to share your personal information in the following situations:

  • AI Service Providers. Receipt images are sent to Anthropic (Claude API) for processing. Images are used solely to extract core charge data and are not stored permanently by Anthropic.
  • Business Transfers. We may share or transfer your information in connection with any merger, sale, financing, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

4. DO WE OFFER ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE-BASED PRODUCTS?

In Short: Yes. We offer AI-powered receipt scanning using the Anthropic Claude API.

As part of our Services, we offer products and features powered by artificial intelligence. These tools are designed to enhance your experience by automatically extracting core charge information from receipt images.

AI Service Provider: Anthropic (Claude API)

AI Product Function: Image analysis — extracting core charge amounts, store names, part names, and return deadlines from receipt photos.

How We Process Your Data Using AI: Receipt images are transmitted securely via HTTPS to the Anthropic API, processed to extract text data, and the extracted data is returned to your device. Images are not stored permanently. All extracted data (amounts, dates, store names) is saved locally on your device only.

You must not use our AI Products in any way that violates the terms or policies of Anthropic.

5. HOW LONG DO WE KEEP YOUR INFORMATION?

In Short: We keep your information only as long as necessary.

All core deposit data is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain a central database of user information. When you delete the CoreCash app, all locally stored data is removed from your device.

Receipt images are processed in real time and are not stored by us or permanently retained by our AI service provider.

6. HOW DO WE KEEP YOUR INFORMATION SAFE?

In Short: We aim to protect your personal information through organizational and technical security measures.

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process, including:

  • All data stored locally on your device — never on external servers
  • Receipt images transmitted to the Anthropic API over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • No user accounts or passwords — no credential data to compromise
  • No central database — no data breach risk from our systems

However, no electronic transmission over the Internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. Use the Services within a secure environment.
